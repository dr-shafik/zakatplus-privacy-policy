Privacy Policy for ZakatPlus

Effective date: 14.12.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ZakatPlus (“the App”) handles your information. ZakatPlus does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter stays entirely on your device.

Information Collection and Use

ZakatPlus is designed to work completely offline. We do not collect or transmit any personal or financial information.

Data You Provide (Stored Locally Only)

All information you enter into the App — including wealth amounts, asset values, and other calculation inputs — is:

  • processed locally on your device
  • stored only on your device
  • never sent to us or any third party

Payment History

The “Payment History” feature saves your Zakat calculation results in a local database on your device. We do not have access to this data.

If you uninstall the App or clear its data, all stored information is permanently deleted.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

ZakatPlus does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective date.”
